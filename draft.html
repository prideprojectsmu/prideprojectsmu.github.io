In Search of Belonging

LGBT Student Organizing in the 1980s and Now

An oral, archival, and living history project created by Pride@SMU. The project in its entirety consists of archival research, oral interviews, a documentary film, a physical exhibit, and in-person social movements.

Timeline

LGBT history on SMU's campus reaches back decades, and often showcases the struggles of queer students to express themselves freely on campus. At the same time, there have been many hard-won victories for queer representation, including the chartering of GLSSO and the establishment of a Queer senator position.
